Campus Life

Oakridge Bachupally

Built in 2001

Oakridge International Schools are part of the world’s leading premium schools organization, Nord Anglia Education. Over the past decade, Oakridge has garnered a strong reputation to become one of the best international schools in the country. The prestigious international school was started with a mission to build a community of leaders, over a foundation of shared beliefs, values and ethos.

Learning Resource Center

Built in 2021

With the Learning Resource Center, Oakridge aims to bring the world of education and literature to the students and furnish their learning experience with advancing technology as well as developing resourceful surrounding that nurtures the intellect of our students has been our main motivation that has helped this project come to fruition.

Cafeteria

Built in 2008

Love food, hate waste. Empowering students to feel knowledgeable and invest their time in acting upon reducing wastage of food in the cafeterias, senior students at the school come forward to create lasting impacts. With a thought of sustainability and food waste reduction, students and teachers were galvanized to work towards mindful eating, as part of the program.
